// about us

Built to run hiring, so you don't have to

Kesendra Workforce is a contract-based workforce partner. Companies bring us a hiring need, and we take on the contract to source, screen, and place the talent — then keep managing the HR side of that placement for as long as the contract runs.

Instead of a company building out a recruitment and HR function from scratch, Kesendra Workforce sits in that gap: sourcing candidates, running AI-assisted screening, managing internship programs, and keeping a standing talent pipeline ready for the next role.
